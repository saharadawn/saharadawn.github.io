La Maison Volpé

A creatively typeset book using the written work of Natalia Ginzburg. Paired with images from @normanscafelondon. Exploring the concept of taste, both in the literal sense–Ginzburg’s critique of British cuisine–but also, ‘taste’ in the sense of, our own cultural biases that we use to judge other cultures.

(3)15 Found Photos and Their Staged Counterparts

A 15 object, 30 photo, digital archive documenting the various random objects that exist in my camera roll, and their studio photographed counterparts. Each object was shot on an iPhone in its “found environment” then reshot in a studio setting Exploring how the percieved value of objects is heavily influenced by the way they are presented before us. Coded, designed, and photographed by me.
